Scenarios

  • The São Paulo Chamber of Commerce staff has extensive experience in receiving businessmen and trade missions from all over the world interested in doing business in Brazil.
  • Through detailed analysis, we provide key strategic information about the Brazilian market, such as the potential for business generation, competition, trends, distribution channels, and others studies according to our client's needs.
  • We can arrange meetings with Brazilian entrepreneurs and market specialists, technical visits to industries, shopping centers, retailers, industry and trade associations, chambers of commerce and, in some cases, the major trade shows and fairs in Brazil.
  • We also provide all necessary infrastructure, such as event venues, translators, transportation and hotels, among others. Send us a message and schedule a meeting with our team.
